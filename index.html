PENTAGON FINANCE

What is $PENT ?

PENT is the token that powers
the Pentagon Finance protocol.
The PENT token enables the
creation/fusion of nodes and
gives rights to vote on investment
and development proposals.
PENT tokens are issued as a
reward for creating nodes.

Why $PENT ?

  • • Team KYC’s by AssureDeFi
  • • Creating / Fusing Nodes
  • • Compound your rewards
  • • Benefits all participants
  • • Lower and more sustainable rewards
  • • Burn mechanics for more stability
  • • Quick and cheap transactions
  • • Cheap entry cost

Creating/Fusing Nodes

Pentagon nodes are what enable
users to generate passive income.
Users can choose between different
tiers of nodes, all with different
rewards and upfront costs.
Once users accumulate a certain
amount of nodes, they will be able
to fuse their nodes to more powerful
ones.

Compound your rewards

The Pentagon Finance system is
designed to incentivize compounding
your rewards for maximum income.
By continuously creating and fusing
nodes your rewards increase exponentially,
and you will in turn achieve powerful
passive income.
